## Platforms
[Parent Page](Home)

Platforms are the environments where AI-powered work is executed.

**Models provide intelligence.  
Platforms provide workflow.**

In this training program, platforms are treated as structured work surfaces — not as novelty tools. Each serves a specific role in the cognitive stack.

---

# Knowledge Platforms

These platforms store, organize, and structure information over time.

---

## NotebookLM (NLM)

- **Owner:** Google  
- **Best Use:** Structured research, document-grounded analysis, and source-based synthesis  
- **Description:** NotebookLM is a research-focused AI platform that works directly from uploaded documents and sources. It is designed for grounded reasoning, source citation, and structured synthesis.  

NotebookLM is particularly useful for:
- Research consolidation  
- Multi-document analysis  
- Building structured study or briefing notebooks  
- Creating source-backed summaries  

It emphasizes grounding in uploaded material rather than free-form generation.

---

## Notion

- **Owner:** Notion Labs  
- **Best Use:** Structured knowledge management and AI-accessible databases  
- **Description:** Notion is a flexible workspace combining documents and databases. It serves as a persistent knowledge layer where structured information, workflows, and AI-assisted documentation can live long term.  

Notion is often used for:
- Project tracking  
- Knowledge repositories  
- Structured operating documents  
- Living documentation systems  

It provides continuity — something chat interfaces alone do not.

# Productivity Platforms

These are execution environments where financial, analytical, and presentation work happens.

---

## Excel / PowerPoint

- **Owner:** Microsoft  
- **Best Use:** Financial modeling and professional communication  
- **Description:** Excel and PowerPoint remain industry standards for financial analysis and presentation. Increasingly augmented by AI copilots, they are core execution tools in commercial real estate and enterprise environments.  

Excel is used for:
- Financial models  
- Sensitivity analysis  
- Data manipulation  
- Scenario modeling  

PowerPoint is used for:
- Investment memos  
- Market presentations  
- Structured deal communication  

AI enhances these tools but does not replace financial discipline.

---

## Google Workspace (Docs / Sheets / Slides)

- **Owner:** Google  
- **Best Use:** Collaborative document workflows  
- **Description:** Google’s productivity suite integrates AI assistance directly into document creation and collaboration. It is commonly used in organizations standardized on Google infrastructure.

# Communication & Workflow Systems

AI increasingly embeds into communication platforms. These systems form the operational backbone of most organizations.

Examples include:

- **Email platforms** (Outlook, Gmail)  
- **Slack**  
- **Microsoft Teams**  
- **Monday.com**  
- **Asana**  
- **Other project management systems**

These platforms are not intelligence engines themselves, but AI often operates inside them.

They are used for:
- Task coordination  
- Internal communication  
- Project tracking  
- Decision documentation  

AI in these systems must be governed carefully to avoid information leakage or process drift.

---

# Platform Philosophy

We distinguish clearly between:

- **Models** → Intelligence engines  
- **Platforms** → Work environments  
- **Knowledge systems** → Persistence and continuity  
- **Communication systems** → Operational coordination  

Effective AI adoption depends less on choosing the “best model” and more on selecting the right platform for the task.

---

# Starting Recommendation

New users should:

1. Choose one knowledge platform (NotebookLM or Notion).  
2. Use Excel or Sheets for structured numerical work.  
3. Use AI inside one productivity tool weekly.  
4. Maintain documentation of assumptions and decisions.  
5. Expand into advanced platforms (Antigravity, Cowork) only after mastering fundamentals.

AI works best when layered onto disciplined workflows — not when scattered across tools.
